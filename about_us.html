ABOUT THE TEAM

Lynbrook Wrestling is a team based in San Jose, California, wrestling in the upper division of the Santa Clara Valley Athletic League. The team consists of both varsity and JV teams, and has a diverse variety of members. Practices are mainly in the Winter sports season from November to February, every weekday evening from 4:30 to 6:30. However, there are off-season practices; these will be posted on the team Facebook as well as the calendar, both of which are attached to the site. Join, and become part of our family!

WHY JOIN?

When a student joins Lynbrook Wrestling, they become a part of a supportive, cultivating, and loving community. Tightly-knit, this community will help you grow both on and off the mat. Furthermore, you will be able to learn wrestling technique from phenomenal technicians, train with great practice partners, and compete in tournaments. Wrestling is a life skill, fundamental to the combat sports, and a great way to learn discipline, mental toughness, and the capacity to never give up.
